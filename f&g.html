    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is COBANG ARK?

    COBANG ARK is an interactive e-learning platform for individuals and organizations which help them gain practical skills for personal or organizational development. Embedded in our program is an automatic rewards system designed to help every student receives financial support as the put their newly acquired skills to the test. This support will help one reach personal and professional goals in life while creating impact in our society.

    Who can join the Cobang Ark community?

    Any person(s) above 18 years who agrees and accepts the terms and conditions of the Cobang Ark educational platform. How do I join the Cobang ARK platform? You can only join by purchasing any of our packages, through the referral link from a registered member or directly through the website.

    What makes up a package?

    Our packages are made up of a combination of ebooks, course materials and video tutorials.

    Can I access my paid materials anything I like?

    Yes, you can. You can access any purchased materials in your library whenever you like on any device.

    Can I purchase more than one package at a time?

    Yes, you can. You can always access your courses at your convenience. Study at your pace.

    How do I see my rewards?

    You can access your rewards through the dashboard by logging in to your account.

    When can I withdraw my rewards?

    Your rewards build up over time. However, you can withdraw your rewards at each complete level. Your first withdr awal starts at level 2 and your last level 4.

    How long does it take to complete level 4?

    Time is irrelevant in our rewards system. However, you can get to level 4 before or after 4 weeks. This is partially dependant on your efforts and others ahead of you as ambassadors. Determination and commitment are your catalyst to a success.

    Who are the Cobang Ark Ambassadors?

    Cobang Ark Ambassadors are members of the educational community who are promoters of Cobang Ark. They share information about Cobang ARK, and help new members have understanding on how our educational program works.

    Can we have Zoom meetings?

    Yes, any Cobang Ark Ambassadors can conduct their own zoom meetings for their team or join any scheduled zoom meetings to understand how this works. Slides are available to them upon request.

    How can I make withdrawals?

    Withdrawals can be done peer to peer, bank transfers or through supported crypto wallets. More withdrawal options will be made available over time to support more counties.

    Can I transfer my rewards to another student within the platform?

    Yes ,you can. You can make a pee-to–pee transfer to another student within the community and receive actual cash.

    What is my role as an ambassador?

    Share approved campaigns to help this Ark Cobang creat awareness. Abide by the rules, make new students feel welcome. Answer questions if you feel you can, and respect others. Be responsible for your own success and make this opportunity work for you, while you work as part of a team to help others to succeed too.

    After completing level 4, can I start all over again?

    Yes, you can. You don’t need to register all afresh. All you need to do is start by purchasing another package or upgrade through the store.

    What do I do if I have more questions that are not covered in the FAQ?

    You can chat with our customer service or contact us via email. See contact for more details.
