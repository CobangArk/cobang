    Our Story

    Cobang ark was conceptualized by two friends who wanted an easier way of funding their small business without the hassle of providing collateral. They decided to find a way to help entrepreneurs like them to solve their funding issue and also provide resources needed to support their career while enhancing their skill set.

    Building a skilled-based Ecosystem

    Acquired Reproductive Knowledge we believe is the foundation for any start-up to build upon. By promoting a skilled-base ecosystem, student can interact seamlessly through our platform and teachers can impact knowledge for career advancements.

    Vision

    To create a community of wealthy knowledge-based entrepreneurs focused on creating jobs and employment in our locality for the betterment of the most deprived members of our society.

    Mission

    To build individuals and their businesses through sustainable wealth creating programs using education as catalysts to build a growth-centred ecosystem mutually benefiting for all.
